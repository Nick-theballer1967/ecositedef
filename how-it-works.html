Why MyCoreAI BMS Optimisation Works

A dedicated optimisation layer closes the performance gaps left by static BMS control.

Why BMS performance drifts

  • Fixed schedules miss real occupancy
  • Static tuning ignores weather shifts
  • Seasonal drift erodes efficiency

Continuous optimisation on top of your existing BMS

User benefit

myCoreAI works because it closes the gap between what a BMS is set up to do (static schedules and fixed tuning) and what buildings actually do (changing weather, occupancy, and performance drift).

Sitting on top of your existing BMS, myCoreAI continuously reads live operating data and outcomes, then sends safe, incremental adjustments every 15 minutes within defined comfort and IAQ guardrails. This prevents common sources of waste — overheating, over-ventilation, and simultaneous heating/cooling — while keeping spaces stable and compliant.

The result is simple: lower energy use and carbon, fewer comfort complaints, clearer system insight for engineers, and verified performance evidence — without replacing hardware or disrupting building operations.

  • Adapts to real building behaviour, not assumptions
  • Reduces wasted energy from overheating, over-ventilation, and simultaneous heating/cooling
  • Maintains comfort and compliance using defined constraints
  • Removes comfort complaints by keeping temperatures and air quality stable
  • Prevents performance drift by optimising continuously, not occasionally
  • Provides clear, auditable evidence of savings and performance

The result is definitive energy savings and improved comfort — without replacing systems.
