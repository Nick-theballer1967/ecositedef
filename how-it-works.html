Why MyCoreAI BMS Optimisation Works

A dedicated optimisation layer closes the performance gaps left by static BMS control.

MyCoreAI works because it improves the gaps left by traditional Building Management System (BMS) control. Most BMS strategies rely on fixed schedules and static tuning, which struggle to adapt to real-world changes like weather, occupancy patterns, and seasonal drift.

Continuous optimisation on top of your existing BMS

MyCoreAI sits alongside the existing BMS and continuously identifies the lowest-energy way to maintain comfort and indoor air quality. By analysing live building data and outcomes, it makes small, safe adjustments at regular intervals to keep performance optimised over time.

  • Adapts to real building behaviour, not assumptions
  • Reduces wasted energy from overheating, over-ventilation, and simultaneous heating/cooling
  • Maintains comfort and compliance using defined constraints
  • Removes comfort complaints by keeping temperatures and air quality stable
  • Prevents performance drift by optimising continuously, not occasionally
  • Provides clear, auditable evidence of savings and performance

The result is definitive energy savings and improved comfort — without replacing systems or disrupting building users.
