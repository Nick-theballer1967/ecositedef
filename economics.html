Economics

The myCoreAI Advantage: Positive Economics

Guaranteed positive economics that convert operational efficiency into immediate NOI gains and long-term asset value.

1

Immediate NOI uplift — no CapEx required

myCoreAI continuously analyses building data — including temperatures, airflow, occupancy signals, and weather — and optimises HVAC performance in real time. Typical outcomes include:

  • 20–40% reduction in HVAC energy consumption
  • Lower peak demand and energy volatility
  • Extended equipment life and reduced maintenance spend

Because the subscription fee is lower than verified energy savings, operating costs fall, NOI increases immediately, and cash flow improves without capital investment.

2

Subscription model that de-risks adoption

Traditional building upgrades require upfront capital, long payback periods, and performance uncertainty. myCoreAI’s subscription-based model removes these barriers.

  • Eliminates upfront CapEx
  • Aligns provider incentives with performance outcomes
  • Allows rapid deployment and scaling
  • Enables portfolio-wide rollout without budget reallocation

Asset managers get measurable financial benefit without balance-sheet risk.

3

Improved asset value through higher NOI

Energy savings achieved through myCoreAI flow directly into NOI. When capitalised, even modest improvements in NOI can materially enhance asset value.

  • Consistent reductions in operating costs increase annual cash flow
  • Higher NOI supports stronger valuations at exit
  • Improved income stability strengthens underwriting assumptions

The uplift is structural, not one-off, reinforcing long-term value.

4

Stronger ESG performance with financial discipline

myCoreAI improves sustainability outcomes as a direct consequence of financial optimisation.

  • Lower energy use and carbon emissions
  • Verifiable, auditable performance data
  • Improved comfort and system stability

Owners meet ESG and Net Zero objectives without compromising returns.

5

Future-proofing assets through intelligence, not infrastructure

myCoreAI integrates with existing BMS platforms and infrastructure, so deployment is fast and minimally disruptive.

  • No hardware replacement
  • Minimal operational disruption
  • Rapid onboarding and scalability

Buildings become adaptive assets that respond to changing energy prices, regulations, and occupancy patterns.

The Bottom Line

Immediate financial gain with long-term resilience

myCoreAI converts operational efficiency into immediate financial gain and durable asset value.

  • Net-positive cash flow through a subscription that costs less than the energy it saves
  • Higher NOI without CapEx
  • Improved valuation outcomes through operational resilience
  • ESG improvements that strengthen investment performance

For commercial real estate owners and operators, myCoreAI is a proven optimisation platform that pays for itself — and more — every month.
