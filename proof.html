Evidence-led results

Proof that better HVAC performance does not require compromising building conditions

EcoAI is designed to reduce unnecessary HVAC energy use while maintaining the indoor conditions a building is expected to provide. The aim is not to save energy by allowing temperatures to drift away from requirements. The aim is to operate HVAC systems more intelligently so that buildings maintain required conditions while unnecessary heating and electrical demand are reduced.

This proof page explains how EcoAI demonstrates performance in practice — through operational data, before-and-after analysis and evidence from live building environments.

Savings without sacrificing conditions

One of the most common concerns with any energy-saving solution is whether lower energy use is achieved by allowing comfort conditions to slip. EcoAI is designed specifically to avoid that. The platform works alongside the existing BMS to improve how HVAC systems operate while maintaining the conditions the building is required to provide.

Required conditions maintained

Internal temperature data shows buildings remaining within their required operating range while EcoAI continues to optimise HVAC performance.

Lower energy at comparable temperatures

At similar outdoor temperatures, EcoAI can achieve lower HVAC energy use, showing that savings come from better control and operation rather than reduced standards.

Operational improvement, not compromise

The objective is to reduce unnecessary heating and electrical demand while maintaining building performance expectations, not to cut energy by relaxing the indoor conditions occupants rely on.
