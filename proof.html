MyCoreAI FAQs

Answers to common questions about AI-driven BMS optimisation.

Does MyCoreAI replace our existing BMS?

No. MyCoreAI works with your existing BMS. It does not replace controls, schedules, or hardware — it acts as an intelligent optimisation layer on top of what you already have.

What kind of energy savings are typical?

myCoreAI typically delivers 20–35% energy savings without the need for any manual intervention, depending on building type, usage, and baseline performance.

How is performance measured and verified?

MyCoreAI provides clear before-and-after comparisons, weather-normalised analysis, and auditable performance data. Ongoing monitoring helps ensure savings persist over time.
