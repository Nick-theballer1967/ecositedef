MyCoreAI FAQs

Answers to common questions about AI-driven BMS optimisation.

What is MyCoreAI?

MyCoreAI is an AI-driven building optimisation platform that sits alongside your existing Building Management System (BMS). It continuously optimises HVAC performance to reduce energy consumption and carbon emissions while maintaining comfort and compliance.

Does MyCoreAI replace our existing BMS?

No. MyCoreAI works with your existing BMS. It does not replace controls, schedules, or hardware — it acts as an intelligent optimisation layer on top of what you already have.

How does MyCoreAI reduce energy use?

MyCoreAI continuously analyses live building data (e.g. temperature, humidity, CO₂, setpoints, plant operation, and outcomes) and makes small, safe adjustments at regular intervals.

  • Eliminates overheating and overcooling
  • Reduces simultaneous heating and cooling
  • Improves plant efficiency
  • Adapts to real occupancy and weather conditions
Will this affect occupant comfort?

No — comfort is protected by design. MyCoreAI operates within predefined comfort and air-quality constraints. In many buildings, comfort consistency improves due to reduced temperature swings and better control stability.

How quickly do we see results?

Savings typically begin immediately after optimisation goes live. Meaningful performance evidence is usually available within the first few weeks, with further stabilisation over the following months as the system learns building behaviour.

What level of disruption is required to install MyCoreAI?

Very little. There is no capital replacement, no new controls hardware, and no disruption to building users. Deployment is typically remote following data integration with the existing BMS.

Is MyCoreAI suitable for older buildings?

Yes — particularly so. Older buildings often have the greatest performance gap between “how they run” and “how they could run.” MyCoreAI is designed to optimise existing systems, regardless of building age.

Does MyCoreAI work across multiple buildings?

Yes. MyCoreAI is commonly deployed across portfolios, estates, and campuses, providing consistent optimisation and a unified view of performance across multiple buildings.

How is performance measured and verified?

MyCoreAI provides clear before-and-after comparisons, weather-normalised analysis, and auditable performance data. Ongoing monitoring helps ensure savings persist over time.

What kind of energy savings are typical?

Most customers achieve 20–35% reductions in HVAC energy consumption, depending on building type, usage, and baseline performance.

Is the solution secure?

Yes. MyCoreAI follows industry best practices for data security, access control, and system segregation. It operates within agreed boundaries and does not override safety-critical controls.

How is MyCoreAI paid for?

The service is typically delivered via a subscription model, designed to be self-funding — where energy savings exceed the cost of the service.

Can MyCoreAI support net zero and sustainability targets?

Yes. By reducing operational energy use and Scope 1 & 2 emissions, MyCoreAI provides a fast, low-risk contribution toward net zero targets and ongoing sustainability reporting.
