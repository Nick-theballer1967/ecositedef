Commercial buildings

AI HVAC optimisation for commercial buildings and portfolios

EcoAI helps commercial buildings reduce HVAC energy through existing BMS infrastructure, without replacing plant, disrupting tenants or relying on ongoing manual tuning.

For offices, multi-let buildings, landlords, managing agents, asset managers and FM teams, HVAC optimisation is often a large and underused opportunity. EcoAI delivers myCoreAI as a comfort-first optimisation layer that works through the existing BMS while the BMS remains the control system.

Commercial fit

EcoAI is relevant where existing commercial buildings have suitable BMS access, meaningful HVAC loads and controllable setpoints.

  • Comfort and operations Reduce wasted HVAC energy while protecting tenant comfort and agreed comfort limits.
  • Existing BMS infrastructure Keep the BMS as the control system while myCoreAI provides optimisation above it.
  • Verified reporting Support savings, carbon and operational performance discussions with clearer evidence.

The commercial building challenge

Comfort, cost and carbon all have to be managed at once

Tenant comfort

Commercial buildings need to reduce energy without creating comfort complaints or operational disruption.

Changing occupancy

Office use, weather conditions and internal gains can change throughout the week, making fixed schedules and static settings inefficient.

Energy and ESG pressure

Owners and operators need measurable reductions in consumption, cost and carbon.

Limited time for manual tuning

FM and controls teams cannot manually optimise every setpoint every 15 minutes across a building or portfolio.

How EcoAI helps

Continuous optimisation through the existing BMS

The BMS controls

The existing BMS remains in place and continues to control plant, local loops, schedules and safety functions.

myCoreAI optimises

myCoreAI reads selected BMS data, weather inputs and building behaviour, then calculates optimisation adjustments within agreed comfort and operational limits.

EcoAI delivers and verifies

EcoAI supports UK implementation, configuration, review and reporting so savings can be understood and evidenced clearly.
