Sectors

HVAC optimisation for complex buildings and estates

EcoAI helps organisations reduce HVAC energy across commercial buildings, university estates and other complex BMS-controlled environments, without replacing existing infrastructure.

The opportunity is often found in buildings where HVAC plant, schedules, occupancy, weather and comfort requirements are difficult to optimise manually every day. EcoAI delivers myCoreAI as an optimisation layer through the existing BMS, helping buildings respond more intelligently while the BMS remains the control system.

Sector fit

EcoAI is designed for existing buildings where HVAC performance can be improved through data-led optimisation and agreed comfort limits.

  • Commercial buildings Offices, landlords, managing agents, asset managers and portfolios.
  • Universities Campus estates, technical environments and complex occupied buildings.
  • BMS-controlled buildings Existing systems with suitable access, HVAC loads and controllable setpoints.
